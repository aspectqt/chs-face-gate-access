Institution & Project

Face Recognition Student Gate Access System with SMS Notification for Cawitan High School

Developed by student researchers from Negros Oriental State University – Bayawan–Sta. Catalina Campus, under the College of Arts and Sciences (CAS).

The project is designed to strengthen school security, automate attendance monitoring, and deliver real-time parent notifications through face recognition technology and SMS alerts.

It reflects innovation, research-driven development, and technical collaboration focused on practical, campus-ready digital solutions.
